Who Am I

Born in Kerala, raised in Kolkata, settled in Hyderabad — working as an IT professional for over a decade while building one of South India's most versatile acting careers.

Trained in western and classical dance, a committed fitness enthusiast, trained in horse-riding, an avid reader, and an associate writer with the Telugu Cine Writers' Association. A passionate entrepreneur who brings the same conviction to every frame.
